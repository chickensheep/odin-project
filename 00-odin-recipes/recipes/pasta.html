Pasta

Description

Cheese pasta is a creamy and comforting dish made by combining pasta with a rich, cheesy sauce. Typically prepared with ingredients like milk, butter, and melted cheese, it is a simple yet indulgent meal. Perfect for quick lunches or cozy dinners, cheese pasta is loved for its velvety texture and savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta
  2. Prepare the Sauce Base
  3. Add milk
  4. Melt the Cheese
  5. Combine and serve
